This has reference to your letter dated 15 March 2021 requesting for clarification on the loan agreement between the Land Bank of the Philippines, Tabuk City, Kalinga branch and the Municipal Government of Sabangan, Mountain Province.

Foremost, per Section 22(a) (5)1 and (c) of Republic Act No. 7160 otherwise known as the Local Government Code of 1991 (LGC), local government units (LGUs) have the power to enter into contracts with other entities and these contracts can be in the form of a Memorandum of Agreement (MOA). Subject to the condition, under Section 22(c) of the LGC, that such power to enter into contract can be exercised by the LGU concerned through its local chief executive (LCE) provided that said official has been duly authorized by the sanggunian concerned.

As to the MOA itself, the Department has no authority to declare the same as invalid as the Department is proscribed by its internal rules as provided in Memorandum Circular No. 2010-022 to act on queries of the following kinds, to wit: xxx
