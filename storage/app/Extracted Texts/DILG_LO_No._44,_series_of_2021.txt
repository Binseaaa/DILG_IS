Dear Dir. Ingeniero:
This has reference to your Memorandum dated 27 November 2020, requesting for clarification and guidance in connection with the issuance of EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 187, S. 2020 of the City of Iloilo entitled: AN EXECUTIVE ORDER PROHIBITING THE OPERATIONS OF BEERHOUSES, KARAOKE BARS, AND OTHER SIMILAR ESTABLISHMENTS WHOSE PRIMARY BUSINESS IS THE SERVING OF ALCOHOLIC BEVERAGES AND PROVIDING GUIDELINES FOR ESTABLISHMENTS WHOSE BUSINESS INCLUDE THE SERVING OF ALCOHOLIC BEVERAGES.
At the outset, we would like to stress that this Department is an agency under the executive branch of the government and as such, it has no authority to declare any issuance by the Local Chief Executive in violation of Republic Act No. 7160 otherwise known as the "Local Government Code of 1991" (LGC) or other applicable laws. Only the courts have the authority to do so upon initiation of the appropriate action.
For this reason, we shall discuss only the provisions of law or rules which we deem applicable based on the representations in the letter.
